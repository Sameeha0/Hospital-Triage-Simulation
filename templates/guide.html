How to Play

Game Guide Analytics

This simulation places you in the role of a hospital triage officer during an infectious disease outbreak. Patients arrive one at a time. For each patient you must choose one action: Admit, Discharge, or Isolate.

What you see

  • Age - patient age (1-90)
  • Symptoms - visible symptoms
  • Exposure - recent exposure history
  • Comorbidity - chronic conditions
  • Risk badge - Low/Medium/High

Your Decisions

  • Admit - bring to hospital (uses bed)
  • Discharge - send home (efficient for low-risk)
  • Isolate - isolate and treat (protects staff)

Dashboard Metrics

  • Available beds
  • Day counter
  • Patients treated
  • Deaths & recovered
  • Infected staff
  • Public trust %

End Conditions

Game ends when:

  • Beds reach zero
  • Public trust reaches 0%
  • Day reaches 15

Strategic Tips

  • Balance resource use vs. trust
  • Isolate high-risk patients safely
  • Monitor staff infections
  • Watch trust trends

Design Notes

Self-contained Flask backend with vanilla JS frontend. Deterministic enough to teach trade-offs while remaining engaging.
